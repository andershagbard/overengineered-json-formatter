# Privacy Policy

> We don't collect your data. We don't store your data. We don't want your data.

This extension runs entirely in your browser. It reads JSON from pages you visit, renders it in a nice tree view, and that's it. No data ever leaves your machine. No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking, no servers, no databases. Nothing.

Settings like your preferred theme and tab width are stored locally using Chrome's built-in sync storage - the same way Chrome stores your bookmarks. That data is yours, synced by Chrome to your own Google account if you have sync enabled. We never touch it.

If that still sounds like too much overengineering for a privacy policy, here's the short version: **we don't store or use any user data. At all.**
